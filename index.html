Mini Keyboard with ESP32

Welcome to our Mini Keyboard with ESP32! This is a compact but powerful keyboard that connects easily via USB and Bluetooth. With customization features and the ability to integrate into IoT systems, this product brings flexibility and convenience to users in many different situations.

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Processor: 32-bit Tensilica Xtensa LX6, Dual-core.

  • Storage: 520 KB SRAM, 4 MB or 8 MB Flash.

  • Speed: maximum speed 240 MHz.

  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.2 (Classic and BLE).

  • Protocol: UART, SPI, I2C, I2S, CAN, PWM, 12-bit ADC, 8-bit DAC.

  • Power Supply: 2.2V to 3.6V, USB port for power and programming.

  • Size: Approx 58mm x 31mm.

COMPATIBILITY

OS: Windows, MacOS, Linux.

Port: USB.
