Mini Keyboard

Welcome to our Mini Keyboard with Arduino Pro Micro ATmega32U4! This is a compact but powerful keyboard that connects easily via USB and Bluetooth. With customization features and the ability to integrate into IoT systems, this product brings flexibility and convenience to users in many different situations.

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Processor: ATmega32U4.

  • Storage: 32KB Flash, 2.5KB SRAM, 1KB EEPROM.

  • Speed: 16MHz.

  • Protocol: USB 2.0, I2C, SPI, UART.

  • Power Supply: 5 volts, current consumption approximately 20 milliamperes.

COMPATIBILITY

OS: Windows, MacOS, Linux.

Port: USB.
